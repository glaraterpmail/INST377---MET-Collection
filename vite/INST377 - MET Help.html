Frequently Asked Questions

How do I navigate the website?

Use the navigation menu at the top to explore different sections, including the Home, Browse, Details, About, and Help pages.

How can I search for specific artworks?

Visit the Browse Page and use filters such as region, artist, medium, or time period to discover artworks that match your preferences.

Where can I find detailed information about an artwork?

Click on an artwork from the Browse Page to access the Details Page, which provides comprehensive information, including artist, medium, dimensions, and high-resolution images.

What is the purpose of this platform?

Our goal is to make The Metropolitan Museum of Art’s Asian Art collection accessible to users worldwide by providing an interactive experience with detailed artwork descriptions and curated highlights.

What data sources power this platform?

We use The Met’s Open Access API to retrieve artwork details, department information, and search results related to Asian Art.

For further assistance, please contact our support team.
